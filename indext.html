The Menu

Every dish made fresh, every portion generous. From Texas classics to elevated brunch favorites — there's something for everyone at the table.

Lightly toasted bread with cream cheese icing, fresh strawberries & Nutella. An absolute crowd favorite.

Egg Benedict with Shrimp$17

Poached eggs over crispy fried grits, gulf shrimp & hollandaise. The dish people come back for again and again.

Cajun Chicken & Waffles$16

Crispy seasoned chicken on fluffy waffles with maple syrup and a kick of Cajun spice.

Cowboys Omelette$15

Loaded with brisket, peppers, onions, and cheese. Served with crispy potatoes & toast. Texas approved.

Mickey Pancakes$13

Why We're Different
More Than Just Brunch

We're a family-owned restaurant that opened with one goal — to create an elevated brunch experience where every guest feels right at home. Every dish is made fresh, every portion is generous, and every visit is memorable.

From our Cajun shrimp & grits to our legendary cinnamon roll French toast, we craft every plate with quality ingredients and genuine love. This isn't just breakfast. This is the best part of your day.
